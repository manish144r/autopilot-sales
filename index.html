How it works

Up and running in under 10 minutes

Three steps from sign-up to your first booked meeting.

1

Define your ICP

Tell Autopilot who your ideal customer is — industry, company size, job title, tech stack, signals. No data import needed. The agent builds your list automatically.

2

Set your voice & offer

Share your value proposition, a few examples of emails you love, and what you want prospects to do. The AI matches your tone and adapts every message for each individual lead.

3

Watch meetings appear

Hit go. Your agent starts prospecting and outreaching immediately. Meetings land directly in your calendar. Check the live dashboard to see what's happening in real time.
